> This is a concise signature-oriented draft. It is intentionally general and non-binding. The official text should be reviewed and finalized through the administrative and legal procedures of both institutions.

## Memorandum of Understanding

between

**College of Social Sciences, Jeju National University**

and

**College of Fine Arts and Communication, East Carolina University**

### 1. Purpose

The purpose of this Memorandum of Understanding is to express the mutual interest of the College of Social Sciences at Jeju National University and the College of Fine Arts and Communication at East Carolina University in promoting academic cooperation and educational exchange.

The parties intend to explore opportunities for collaboration in areas of shared academic interest, including communication, media, culture, public relations, strategic communication, arts, design, performance, and related interdisciplinary fields.

### 2. Possible Areas of Cooperation

Subject to mutual agreement and the policies of both institutions, the parties may explore cooperation in the following areas:

1. Faculty academic exchange
2. Student academic exchange
3. Joint lectures, seminars, forums, workshops, or online academic activities
4. Joint student presentations or course-based academic activities
5. Collaborative academic, educational, or cultural programs
6. Other mutually agreed forms of academic cooperation

Specific activities may be developed through separate written arrangements or implementation plans as needed.

### 3. Implementation

Each party may designate one or more coordinators to discuss possible activities under this MOU. The coordinators may communicate regarding schedules, participants, formats, and other practical matters related to proposed activities.

Activities under this MOU shall be implemented only when both parties agree on the relevant details and when the necessary institutional approvals and resources are available.

### 4. Financial Arrangements

This MOU does not create any financial obligation for either party. Each party shall be responsible for its own costs unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Any activity requiring financial support, travel, honoraria, facilities, student support, or other institutional resources shall be subject to separate approval and written agreement by both parties.

### 5. Student and Faculty Mobility

This MOU does not by itself establish a student exchange program, faculty exchange program, credit transfer arrangement, tuition arrangement, or mobility program.

Any activity involving formal enrollment, credit recognition, tuition, visas, housing, insurance, travel, or other mobility-related matters shall follow the relevant policies and procedures of both institutions and may require a separate agreement.

### 6. Intellectual Property and Publicity

Intellectual property, teaching materials, student work, recordings, photographs, publications, or other outputs arising from activities under this MOU shall be handled in accordance with the applicable policies of both institutions and any separate written agreement for the relevant activity.

Use of either party's name, logo, images, or publicity materials shall require prior approval from the relevant party.

### 7. Term

This MOU shall become effective on the date of the final signature by both parties and shall remain in effect for a period of five years, unless terminated earlier in accordance with this MOU.

This MOU may be renewed or extended by mutual written agreement.

### 8. Amendment and Termination

This MOU may be amended by mutual written agreement of both parties.

Either party may terminate this MOU by providing written notice to the other party at least 90 days before the intended termination date. Termination shall not affect activities already in progress unless otherwise agreed by both parties.

### 9. Non-Binding Nature

This MOU is a non-binding statement of mutual interest and intent to explore academic cooperation. It does not create legally binding obligations, financial commitments, exclusive rights, or enforceable duties unless a separate written agreement is executed by both parties.

## 한국어 검토 메모

### 서명용 초안 원칙

- 일반 대학 MOU 관행에 맞춰 포괄적이고 비구속적인 협력 의향서 형태로 작성했다.
- 구체적인 프로그램명, 예산, 방문 일정, 공동센터 모델 등은 본문에서 제외했다.
- 교수 교류, 학생 교류, 공동 세미나, 공동발표회, 공동 프로그램 가능성은 포함하되, 모두 `may explore` 수준으로 표현했다.
- 재정, 학점, 이동, 비자, 보험, 시설, 학생지원 등은 별도 합의가 필요하다고 명시했다.
- 서명 전 양측 공식 영문 명칭, 서명권자, 법무/행정 검토가 필요하다.

### 검토용 초안과의 차이

- [19-mou-draft.md](19-mou-draft.md)는 구체 아이디어와 예시가 포함된 논의용 문서다.
- 이 문서는 실제 서명 가능성을 고려한 간결한 버전이다.
- 세부 실행 아이디어는 MOU 본문이 아니라 별도 implementation plan, appendix, meeting note로 다루는 것이 적절하다.
