# ECU 교수진용 브리핑

> Parent page: [ECU-JNU Culture Exchange Program Overview](../README.md)

## Program Overview

The ECU-JNU Culture Exchange is a student-centered program connecting ECU students with Jeju National University students. The afternoon program is connected to future MOU discussions between the JNU College of Social Sciences and the ECU College of Fine Arts and Communication. Since the ECU dean will not travel with the group, MOU details will be discussed separately through a Zoom meeting with relevant participants.

## Purpose

The program is designed to help students experience Korean campus culture, interact with JNU students, and create a short visual story together. We expect about 10 JNU student participants, so teams will be arranged with one or two JNU students when possible.

## Schedule

| Time | Activity |
|---|---|
| 2:30-2:50 PM | Official welcome and MOU update |
| 2:50-4:00 PM | Interactive lecture: Introduction to FIM and HGN |
| 4:00-5:30 PM | Team photo story exchange |
| 5:30-8:00 PM | Joint dinner |

## Official Welcome

The opening segment marks the formal exchange context between the two colleges and briefly notes the planned MOU discussion process. Faculty are invited to attend the opening and remain for the student exchange program that follows.

## Part 1

Students will be introduced to two simple frameworks:

- FIM: happiness through driving functions, meta functions, and indicators
- HGN: acceptance, transformation, expansion as a story structure

The lecture is interactive and light. It uses examples, images, short games, and pair talk.

## Part 2

Mixed ECU-JNU teams will create a three-photo story:

1. Acceptance
2. Transformation
3. Expansion

Each team will upload photos and captions to a prepared Notion page. Each team is expected to include a JNU student who helps manage the team page.

## Faculty Role

Faculty are welcome to observe, encourage students, and join informal conversations. The activity is designed to be student-led and low-pressure.
